Skat

What is Skat and Tax card

Skat is a name for Danish taxes. They are relatively high but the money from them is used to provide numerous services and benefits. The tax system in Denmark is progressive, which means the more you earn the higher taxes you pay.

The most important thing is that you get a tax card. If you don’t, the employer will have to take 55% off your salary. Tax card is an electronic card that allows your employer to access your tax information. You can check your tax status on your profile at skat.dk (which is set automatically together with your NemID).

How to get it

Step 1

GATHER DOCUMENTATION

  • your signed employment contract
  • your passport or national ID card

    • Step 2

    APPLY ONLINE

    You can apply online here.

    You will also have to fill in a special application called “Application for tax deduction for foreign students or business apprentice in Denmark (04.062)”. You can find it here. You can read more about tax deduction in here.

    Step 3

    WAITING

    The waiting time is approximately 2 weeks. After that your income tax will be deducted automatically from your salary before you receive it.
