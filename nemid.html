NemID

What is NemID

After getting CPR number you can apply for NemID. *(If you don’t have CPR yet you can apply to get a NemID in connection with the CPR number).

NemID is your digital signature that gives you access to public websites, the digital post, online banking etc. It consists of a user ID (number chosen by you during registration), a 4-digit password, and a code card.

For easier use, you can download a NemID app. It’s available both for Iphone and Android here.

You can read more about NemID here.

How to get it

To get NemID, you need to book an appointment in the citizen service first. You can do it here.

Step 1

GATHER DOCUMENTATION

  • ID with a photo (passport, driving licence)
  • one of the following:
    • yellow health card
    • income statement from SKAT
    • your CPR letter together with a certificate of residence

    Step 2

    YOU MIGHT NEED A WITNESS

    You may need a witness to confirm your identity (however that differs from office to office). A witness must:

  • have NemID for at least 30 days
  • be over 18 years old and bring photo ID, NemID and yellow health card
  • not have been registered as a witness for more than 3 times in the past 120 days

    • Changing the address

    You need to inform Danish authorities no later than 5 days after moving to the new address, otherwise you may receive a fine to pay. You can to this online here or (if you don’t have NemID) in your local citizen office.

    Digital post

    It’s an email that Danish government and other public offices will be using to send you information. You need to get a NemID in order to access it. You can log in here (by clicking "Digital Post" icon in the menu).
