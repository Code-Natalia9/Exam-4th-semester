Welcome to HEID!

Hi, I'm Hédi!

H.E.I.D. (Higher Education Infrormations for Denmark) was created with you in mind. My creators had finished their studies and decided to do you a favor. So that you won’t need to go through all the struggle that they went through. In order that you can focus on the important things that made you decide on Denmark in the first place, like studying & making new connections. As for now, we only provide help with AP applications, but when it comes to the registration, we have you covered.
